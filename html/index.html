About the movie

Woz is a private movie about Steve Wozniak, the electronic engineer behind Apple I and Apple II.
About how a young kid ends up building a revolutionary product just for the sake of fun!

The movie will be only released privately to the people involved on it.

This project have no partnership with Wozniak, Woz.org or Wings of Zeus, Inc.
The movie, script and related media is private and can't be shared without or with permission.

If you want to know more you can mail me to eibriel@eibriel.com.
